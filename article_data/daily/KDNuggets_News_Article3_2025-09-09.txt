 # Introduction 
Hugging Face has become the go-to hub for discovering AI models, datasets, and demos. Beyond that, it has also evolved into an educational platform where you can learn through blogs, articles, tutorials, and courses. The community is highly active and interactive, making it a great place to grow your AI knowledge.Hugging FaceHugging FaceIn this article, we’ll review some of the most popular and essential free courses offered on Hugging Face. These courses cover topics such as AI agents, Model Context Protocols (MCPs), large language models (LLMs), diffusion models, and reinforcement learning, all of which are at the core of today’s rapidly evolving AI landscape. # 1. AI Agents Course 
The AI Agents Course is a community-driven program that takes you from theory to practice. You will learn core concepts, build with smol-agents, LlamaIndex, and LangGraph, experiment in preconfigured Hugging Face Spaces, share on the Hub, and compete on a public leaderboard.AI Agents Coursesmol-agentssmol-agentsLlamaIndexLlamaIndexLangGraphLangGraph

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) Course is designed to take you from the basics of AI to advanced mastery of the Model Context Protocol. Throughout the course, you will learn about MCP in theory, design, and practice. Additionally, you can share your projects and explore applications created by the community.Model Context Protocol (MCP) Course

 The course combines foundational units on MCP concepts, architecture, and core components with hands-on building sessions and real-world assignments that you can choose. # 3. Large Language Model Course 
The Hugging Face LLM Course bridges foundational natural language processing (NLP) and the latest advancements in large language models (LLMs), guiding you from essential NLP concepts to hands-on experience with powerful models like those in the Transformers library.Hugging Face LLM CourseTransformersTransformers

 You will learn to use, fine-tune, and deploy LLMs, tackle classic and advanced NLP tasks, and showcase your work — all while building practical skills that prepare you to excel in the rapidly evolving field of AI. # 4. Diffusion Models Course 
The Hugging Face Diffusion Course is a hands-on program that guides you through the theory and practical applications of diffusion models, including generating images and audio with the Diffusers library, training your own models from scratch, fine-tuning existing models on new datasets, and exploring advanced techniques like conditional generation and custom pipelines.Hugging Face Diffusion CourseDiffusersDiffusers

The Hugging Face Deep RL Course offers a comprehensive, hands-on journey through both the theory and practice of deep reinforcement learning (Deep RL), guiding you from foundational concepts to state-of-the-art methods.Hugging Face Deep RL Course
